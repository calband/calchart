HSDM

Format: HSDM <destination>

HSDM marches a two-part course to <destination>. First, the direction will be N, E, W, or S, and stepsize will be HS. Then, the direction will be NW, NE, SE, or SW, and stepsize will be DM. The specific directions are selected so that the path traveled is as short as possible.

This procedure is similar to FOUNTAIN, but HSDM does not require explicit directions. It is also similar to DMHS.
