HSCM

Format: HSCM <point 1> <point 2> <beats>

HSCM executes a high-step countermarch in the Cal Band style.

The movement commences at <point 1> by moving one step either N or S, then moving two steps E or W, then moving opposite to the first direction until reaching <point 2>, then continuing one step in the same direction, then moving the two steps E or W, then in the initial direction until <point 1> is reached again.

This pattern is continued until the specified number of beats have been used.

The upper-right and lower-left corners of the countermarch block should be specified with reference points.

A high-step countermarch of length 16 and R1 and R2 as the two corners would look like this: 

  HSCM R1 R2 16
This is a specialized case of COUNTERMARCH, as is DMCM.
