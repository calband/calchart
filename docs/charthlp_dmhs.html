DMHS

Format: DMHS <destination>

DMHS marches a two-part course to <destination>. First, the direction will be NW, NE, SW, or SE, and stepsize will be DM. Then, the direction will be N, S, E, or W, and stepsize will be HS. The specific directions are selected so that the path traveled is as short as possible.

This procedure is similar to FOUNTAIN, but DMHS does not require explicit directions. It is also similar to HSDM.
