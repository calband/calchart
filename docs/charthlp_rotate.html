ROTATE

Format: ROTATE <angle> <steps> <pivot point>

ROTATE will rotate P around the <pivot point> for the given number of steps. The total angle traversed will be <angle> (given in degrees).

Note that angle is interpreted in the standard mathematical way: Positive angles rotate counter-clockwise Negative angles rotate clockwise

Another note: If <pivot point> coincides with P, then the person will rotate around himself (i.e. stand in one place and turn slowly). The person will begin the rotation facing DOH.
